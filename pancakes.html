Pancakes Recipe

  • Home

Description

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch, ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and butter.
  3. Stir until just combined.
  4. Pour batter onto hot greased pan, cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.
  5. Serve warm with syrup or fruit.
