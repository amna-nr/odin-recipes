Cake Recipe

Home

Description

A quick, soft, and rich chocolate cake made with basic pantry ingredients. Perfect for when you're short on time but want something delicious.

Ingredients

  • Flour 1 cup
  • Sugar 1 cup
  • Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
  • Baking powder 1 tsp
  • Eggs 2
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Oil 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla essence 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Whisk until smooth with no lumps.
  5. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
  6. Check with a toothpick — if it comes out clean, it's done.
  7. Let the cake cool before cutting or frosting.
