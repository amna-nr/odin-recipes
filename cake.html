Description

A quick, soft, and rich chocolate cake made with basic pantry ingredients. Perfect for when you're short on time but want something delicious.

Ingredients

  • Flour 1 cup
  • Sugar 1 cup
  • Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
  • Baking powder 1 tsp
  • Eggs 2
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Oil 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla essence 1 tsp
