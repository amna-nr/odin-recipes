Description

A classic and comforting red sauce pasta made with a rich, tangy tomato base, sautéed garlic, onions, and Italian herbs. This flavorful sauce coats tender pasta perfectly and can be customized with vegetables, cheese, or your favorite protein. It's an easy, delicious meal ready in under 30 minutes — perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta 1 cup (penne, spaghetti, or your choice)
  • Tomatoes 2 large (blended or finely chopped)
  • Garlic 2 cloves (chopped)
  • Olive oil or any cooking oil 1 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Red chili flakes or powder to taste
  • Oregano or mixed herbs optional (½ tsp)
